Due to ongoing federally mandated safety improvements (Positive Train Control), there will be system wide service changes in effect throughout 2018, including 17 weekend closures. For information about the 2018 weekend closures, click here.

To help riders stay on track during this time, PATH will provide a free 2-Trip MetroCard for transfer to MTA Subway connections. The 2-Trip MetroCards will be distributed by PATH employees at World Trade Center Station ONLY from 11:59 p.m. Friday through 5 a.m. Monday and must be obtained before exiting the turnstiles at World Trade Center Station. These MetroCards will only be valid during the weekend they are issued.

Throughout these weekend tunnel closures, please allow additional travel time.

We thank you for your patience.
