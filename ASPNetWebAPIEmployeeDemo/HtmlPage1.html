Due to ongoing Positive Train Control (PTC) testing, weekend tunnel closures will be in effect through October 2018.

To accommodate riders during this time, PATH is providing a free 2-Trip MetroCard for uptown travel. The 2-Trip MetroCards will be distributed by PATH employees at WTC Station from 11:59 p.m. Friday through 5 a.m. Monday each weekend and must be obtained before exiting the turnstiles at WTC Station. These MetroCards will only be valid during the weekend they were issued.

Throughout these weekend tunnel closures, please allow additional travel time.

We thank you for your patience.
